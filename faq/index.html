Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is PDF Scanner?

PDF Scanner is a mobile app that allows you to quickly scan documents using your phone’s camera, converting them into high-quality PDFs that are easy to save, share, or print.

2. How do I scan a document?

To scan a document, open the app, align the document in the camera view, and tap the scan button. You can then adjust and crop the image before saving it as a PDF.

3. Can I save multiple pages in a single PDF?

Yes, our app allows you to create multi-page PDFs. Simply scan each page, and the app will combine them into a single file.

4. What features are included in the free version?

The free version includes essential scanning features and basic document saving options.

5. What additional features are available with the premium version?

With a premium subscription, you gain access to ad-free scanning, advanced editing tools, and unlimited PDF exports.

6. How do I subscribe to the premium version?

To subscribe, go to the app's settings, select "Upgrade to Premium," and follow the prompts to complete your purchase.

7. How can I manage or cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription through your device’s App Store settings. Go to "Subscriptions" to make changes or cancel.

8. How do I ensure my scans are secure?

Your scans are securely saved only on your device.

9. How do I contact customer support?

If you have questions or need assistance, reach out to our support team at Karamuratt123@icloud.com. We’re here to help!

10. Can I recover deleted scans?

Once a scan is deleted, it is permanently removed from the app. We recommend using cloud backup for important documents.

11. How do I improve scan quality?

For best results, scan documents in a well-lit environment. The app includes tools for adjusting brightness and contrast to optimize scan quality.

12. Can I share my PDFs directly from the app?

Yes, you can share PDFs via email, messaging apps, or cloud storage directly from the app for easy collaboration and sharing.

If you have any other questions, please contact our support team for further assistance.
